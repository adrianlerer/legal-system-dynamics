## Writing Guidelines

### Style
- **Accessible**: No jargon without explanation
- **Examples**: Real cases (Argentina, Hungary, USA)
- **Visuals**: At least one figure per post
- **Length**: 2,500‚Äì3,500 words (5‚Äì8 minute read)
- **Tone**: Rigorous but conversational

### Structure
1. **Hook**: Surprising fact or provocative question
2. **Context**: Brief background
3. **Core Idea**: Main concept explained
4. **Evidence**: Data, cases, experiments
5. **Implications**: So what? Why does it matter?
6. **Call to Action**: Read paper, explore data, subscribe

### Linking Strategy
- **Internal**: Link to papers, notebooks, data
- **External**: Link to academic sources, news articles
- **Cross-posts**: Reference previous Substack articles
